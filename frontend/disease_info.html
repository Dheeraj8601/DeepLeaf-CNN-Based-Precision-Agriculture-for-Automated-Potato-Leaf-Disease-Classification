Late Blight (Phytophthora infestans)

1. Symptoms:

  • Dark green to brown lesions on leaves, usually starting from the tips.
  • White, fuzzy growth (spores) on the undersides of leaves.
  • Rapid spread during periods of cool, wet weather.

2. Solutions:

  • Fungicides: Apply fungicides preventively, especially during periods of high humidity and cool temperatures. Copper-based fungicides are commonly used for late blight control.
  • Resistant Varieties: Choose potato varieties that are resistant to late blight.
  • Crop Rotation: Avoid planting potatoes in the same location for consecutive years.
  • Timely Harvest: Harvest potatoes promptly to minimize exposure to late blight.

Early Blight (Alternaria solani)

1. Symptoms:

  • Dark, concentric rings with a target-like appearance on lower leaves.
  • Yellowing and browning of affected leaves, starting from the bottom of the plant.
  • Reduced yield and quality of potatoes.

2. Solutions:

  • Fungicides: Apply fungicides early in the season and continue at regular intervals, especially during wet periods.
  • Crop Rotation: Practice crop rotation to reduce the build-up of the pathogen in the soil.
  • Mulching: Use organic mulch to reduce soil splashing onto lower leaves, which can carry the fungal spores.
  • Proper Spacing: Plant potatoes with adequate spacing to improve air circulation and reduce humidity around the plants.
  • Pruning: Remove lower infected leaves to prevent the spread of the disease.

General Disease Management Practices

Sanitation:

  • Remove and destroy infected plant material to prevent the spread of spores.
  • Avoid composting infected plant debris unless you have a high-temperature composting system.

Water Management:

  • Water the plants at the base to avoid wetting the foliage, as both diseases thrive in humid conditions.

Monitoring:

  • Regularly inspect plants for symptoms and take action at the first sign of disease.

Resistant Varieties:

  • When available, choose potato varieties that are resistant or tolerant to both late and early blight.
